Services  
Describe the specific project or services to be provided pursuant to this SOW:  
Bread Financial is the Sponsor Bank for multiple Fintech’s. Onboarding, governance and ensuring regulatory 
compliance is essential to the success of this program. In order to collect information from the Fintech’s and ensure 
regular audit of the processes and policies, Bread Financial would like to setup a collaboration platform for these 
Fintech’s.  
The Aethereus team would be responsible for the below: 
 
• 
Setup an onboarding and governance platform for FinTech's and Bread to collaborate and govern 
compliance 
• 
Compliance stages to be tracked for Due Diligence, Pre-Launch testing and First Events Validation.  
• 
Ability to upload documents and comments per item in the due – diligence questionnaire 
• 
Ability to track pre-launch testing & First Events Validation 
• 
Ability to create testing schedules automatically for monitoring compliance 
• 
Provide a workflow to track and report on changes made to policies 
• 
Central hub for policies, training/enablement (incl. SOPs), audits, due diligence artifacts etc. 
• 
Access Control for internal / external users on ability to only see information relevant to them 
• 
Dashboard on fintech progress by opportunity stage, overdue tasks, task completion report 
• 
Creation of complaints cases, routing and resolution workflow 
• 
Build the CRMA dashboard for reviewing information related to Complaints, Applications, Transactions 
• 
Ingest data from Snowflake into Data Cloud for application information

Critical Success Factors  
 
The following factors will be used to determine the success of each release: 
1.1. ON TIME DELIVERY 
 
1.1.1. Release milestones are met including all agreed upon scope identified during release planning efforts. 
Note that any dependencies that Aethereus cannot control and aren’t responsible for are legitimate 
reasons to re-evaluate and re-baseline release milestones. 
1.2. QUALITY 
 
1.2.1. 100% Unit Test Pass Percentage for each release 
1.2.2. 99% Functional Test Execution Percentage for each release 
1.2.3. 95% Functional Test Pass Percentage for each release 
1.2.4. 80% Test Automation coverage for components that are automatable for each release 
1.2.5. No open Critical or High defects at the completion of UAT for each release. This category is defined as 
the loss of one or more major features in the application. The feature is unusable. 
 
1.3 All code, including automated tests, are approved by Comenity Salesforce Support team members.

Acceptance Criteria  
 
The acceptance criteria for each of the Deliverables hereunder will be that such Deliverables materially conform to 
the mutually agreed written specifications such that there are no critical defects (“Acceptance Criteria”). Client will 
review each Deliverable and confirm whether it has accepted such Deliverable based on the applicable Acceptance 
Criteria within ten (10) business days after receipt thereof (“Acceptance Period”). If Client does not respond within 
the Acceptance Period, the Deliverables will be deemed accepted.  
 
During the Acceptance Period, Client may make a written request for changes to correct deviations from the 
Acceptance Criteria with sufficient detail to enable Aethereus to pursue an appropriate work-around of the 
Deliverable. Upon receipt of such notice from Client, Aethereus shall use commercially reasonable efforts within 
fifteen (15) calendardays without charge to Client to resolve the failure of the Deliverables and the Deliverable (if 
the error is from any of the environment that Aethereus is supporting) to operate in accordance with any such 
Acceptance Criteria. Any Deliverable that is resubmitted to Client shall be subject to a three (3) business day 
Acceptance Period. If such correction is not made within the 15-day period and the breach is not or cannot be 
cured by Aethereus within such cure period, in addition to all other remedies available to Client, Comenity will have 
the option to terminate the SOW in accordance with termination provisions of the Agreement
